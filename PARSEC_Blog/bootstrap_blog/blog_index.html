About Us
Propulsion, Aeronautics, and Rocket Systems at Caltech is an undergraduate student organization to provide students with a hands-on, research-focused experience in rocket design. Moreover, we are committed to offering students opportunities to learn from a network of expert mentors within the Caltech/JPL community. For our first long-term project, we are building an ambitious autonomous rocket to compete for the FAR-MARS prize in the spring of 2019.
